Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and has since grown into a premier global event for technology leaders and enthusiasts. Each year, the conference evolves to reflect the changing landscape of innovation.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to empower the global tech community by sharing knowledge, building connections, and promoting cutting-edge research and development. We believe in inclusivity, sustainability, and open innovation.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed some of the brightest minds in technology:

  • Dr. Elena Torres – AI researcher and author of “Ethical Machines”.
  • Marcus Lee – CTO of NextGen Robotics, pioneer in automation.
  • Ada Njeri – Cybersecurity evangelist and founder of SafeNet.
